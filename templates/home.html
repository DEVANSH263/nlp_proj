Key Features

Multilingual Input

Accepts both pure English and Hinglish (code-mixed Hindi+English) text, making it suitable for real-world social media content.

Transliteration Normalization

Fuzzy-matches Romanized Hindi tokens to English equivalents (≥85% threshold) before classification, reducing vocabulary mismatch.

Detailed PDF Reports

Generate professional PDF reports per prediction or as a full session summary — including charts, insights, and normalization analysis.
